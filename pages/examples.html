write your HTML and press one of the buttons. see execAll() and execLAyer() explanation on the wiki

code:
write you HTML code here , and click one of the buttons. you can also click `copy` on one of the examples to copy it.
result: 
result shows here.
live result:
here you can see the result live.

simple examples:

example #1 (repeater): copy

write this in your html: 

<repeater times="3">
    <p>this will be repeated 3 times</p>
</repeater>

example #2 (jtag): copy

write this in your html: 

<jtag code="var html=$(this).html();
            $(this).html(html.replace(/blue/gi, 'red'));
            $(this).jend();"> 
    Mr Blue has a blue house and a blue car 
</jtag>

example #3 (fragments): copy

write this in your html: 

<define-fragment name="post">
    <div class="post">
        User: {%username [annoymous]%}<br />
        <p>
            {%text [no text available] %}
        </p>
    </div>
</define-fragment>

<fragment name="post" username="John Connor" text="I've just invented Skynet !"></fragment>
